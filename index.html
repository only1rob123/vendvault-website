Setup in minutes

Three steps.
Then it runs itself.

1

Create your VendVault account

Sign up in 30 seconds. You'll instantly get your unique import email address — something like a3f9b2c1@import.vendvault.net

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do I have to stop using Cantaloupe?

No. VendVault works alongside Cantaloupe, not instead of it. Cantaloupe still handles your telemetry and cashless payments. VendVault just makes the reporting and management side actually usable.

How does the Cantaloupe import work exactly?

You set up a scheduled "Transaction Line Item Export" report in Cantaloupe Seed's Report Scheduler. You point it at your unique VendVault import email address. Every morning Seed emails the report, VendVault picks it up automatically and updates your dashboard. It's a one-time 5-minute setup.

What counts as a "machine"?

Any vending machine you add to VendVault counts. The first 4 are included in the base price. Additional machines are $2/month each. If you have seasonal machines you can deactivate them and not be billed.

Is my sales data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored in an isolated database partition — no other company can see your data. Your import email address is a unique private token. All connections use TLS encryption. We never sell or share your data.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, anytime. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period. We can also export all your data if you want it.
